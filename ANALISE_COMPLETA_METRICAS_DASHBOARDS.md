### **M√©tricas Operacionais:**

```sql
-- Appointment Success Rate

  (COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE status = 'completed') * 100.0 / COUNT(*)) as success_rate
FROM appointments

```

### **M√©tricas de IA:**
```sql

  tenant_id,
```
